The Need For Apply Myself

We are looking to help University students that are looking to apply for internship or graduate jobs to expand their work experience. Currently, students aim to apply for multiple job roles at one point. However, most job applications are extensive and tend to ask the same questions, which students may find disheartening and lose motivation. Many students give up completing applications because it can be so tedious answering the same questions constantly, which could eventually impact their self-confidence.

This way, students are confident that they have showcased their potential to the best of their abilities and have a higher chance of progressing in application processes because they have applied to numerous roles.
