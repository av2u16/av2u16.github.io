APPLY MYSELF

THE NEED FOR APPLY MYSELF

Apply Myself is mainly aimed at the student community. We are looking to help University students that are looking to apply for internship or graduate jobs to expand their work experience. Currently, students aim to apply for multiple job roles at one point. However, most job applications are extensive and tend to ask the same questions, which students may find disheartening and lose motivation. Many students give up completing applications because it can be so tedious answering the same questions constantly, which could eventually impact their self-confidence.

Our initial market research involved 96 participants, of which

  1. 93.8% from our survey said they would find a service like ours useful when applying to jobs
  2. We also explored their current process for job applications and found that 70.8% of participants applied for 0-10 jobs since the start of the academic year
  3. 43.7% said they have left an application incomplete due to procrastination/unmotivated to complete
